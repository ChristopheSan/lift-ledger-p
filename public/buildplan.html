Build a Plan

Instructions:
  • Enter in the details describing your plan.
  • Once you select the number of training days, you can start designing the days
  • Note: modifying # of training days will reset your plan
  • In each column, select "ADD A NEW EXERCISE" to add exercises to each day
  • If you want to remove a specifc exercise click the trash icon
  • To specify the sets/reps of each day, expand each card and fill in the details for each day
  • Once you have filled out every day, navigate to the bottom of the page a click "Save Plan" and you will be redirected to view all plans
